Vega

  • As a result of its brightness and close proximity
    • Vega is the most studied star apart from the sun.
  • In 10,000 years
    • Vega will replace Polaris as the northern pole star.
  • Vega rotates extremely quickly
    • Once every 12 and a half hours compared to the 28 days it takes the sun to rotate on its axis.
  • Vega's rapid rotation causes the star to bulge considerably at its equator.
  • Vega is a bluish-white star with a radius of around 1.1 million miles (1.8 million km)
    • Which is around two and a half times the size of the sun.
